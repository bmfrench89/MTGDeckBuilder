# Deck: Cosmic Spider-Man — 5-color (WUBRG) Spider typal aggro — v1 DRAFT
# Commander: Cosmic Spider-Man  ({W}{U}{B}{R}{G}, 5/5)
#   Grants your OTHER Spiders flying, first strike, trample, lifelink & haste
#   during combat on your turn. Go wide with Spiders, then alpha-strike.
#
# BUILD NOTES / HONESTY:
# - 100% owned cards. The evergreen shell (ramp, fixing, removal, draw, anthems)
#   is verified staples I know cold. The Spider CREATURES are from Marvel's
#   Spider-Man (SPM/PSPM/MSC/MSH) — post-2025 — so their exact type lines are
#   REASONED + spot-verified, not fully confirmed. Load the card-attribute CSV
#   (with Type/Subtype columns) to confirm every one is a Spider and refine.
# - Excluded despite "Spider" in the name (not Spider creatures): Web-Shooters &
#   Spider-Suit are Equipment (kept as gear), Spider-Mobile is a Vehicle,
#   Origin/Pictures/No More/Superior Foes of Spider-Man are enchantments, Web Up
#   / Silk Net are instants. Morlun (anti-Spider Villain) left out on purpose.
# - Green is thin in the collection, so the base leans on rainbow fixing + green
#   ramp to support the many green Spiders/tokens.
# - Top-played Spiders you DON'T own (buy-list): Gwenom, Remorseless; Superior
#   Spider-Man. Both are cheap upgrades.
